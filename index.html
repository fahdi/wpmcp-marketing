The problem
01

AI is confident, not careful.

An AI assistant editing your live store does not pause before a risky change. It moves with the same confidence whether it is fixing a typo or wiping out your homepage.

02

Every other AI tool asks you to just trust it.

Trust is not a safety net. AI is good, not perfect, and one bad change on your storefront is a lost sale and a stressful afternoon you cannot get back.

03

A backup from last night will not save you.

A nightly backup does not help when the AI breaks something ninety seconds ago. You need protection at the moment of the change, not the moment of the night.

How it works

Nothing reaches your store without a copy saved first.

Every change the AI makes, a new product, an edited page, a pricing update, follows the same four steps before it counts as done. You never have to trust that it went well. You can check, and undo it if it did not.

01

Save a copy

Before anything changes, Dukandar quietly saves exactly how that page, product, or setting looked, so there is always a way back.

02

Make the change

The AI does what you asked: update copy, add a product, adjust a setting. One clean change, nothing left half-done.

03

Check the result

A quick check confirms the page still works. If something looks broken, it gets reversed automatically before a customer ever sees it.

04

Undo, any time

Change your mind later? Restore one change, or roll back an entire AI session, from a single screen in your dashboard.

Under the hood

For the technically curious: what the AI can actually touch.

Dukandar is built on the plugin wpmcp, which speaks the Model Context Protocol (MCP), so it works with Claude, Cursor, or any compatible AI client. This table is the full list of what it can read, change, and undo. If you just want the reassurance, you do not need to read it, it is here for developers and agencies who like to check under the hood.

CapabilityTypeWhat it does
get-pagereadRead a page's title, content, and whether it is an Elementor page
update-blockssafe changeReplace a page's content, saved and checked automatically
create-postnew contentCreate a post, page, or product, with categories and details
get-postreadRead a page or product's full content, status, and image
update-postsafe changeUpdate part of a page or product: fields, categories, or image
delete-postsafe on forceMove a page or product to trash by default, or delete permanently with confirmation
list-postsreadSearch or list pages and products by type, status, or text
set-post-termssafe changeAssign categories or tags: replace, add, or remove
get-mediareadRead a photo or file's details: size, dimensions, alt text
update-mediasafe changeUpdate a photo or file's title, alt text, or caption
delete-mediasafe changeDelete a photo or file. Off by default, needs explicit confirmation
sideload-imagenew contentPull an image from a URL into your media library as a new file
get-settingsreadRead store and site settings with their current values
update-settingssafe changeUpdate allowed settings, applying what works even if part of a batch fails
list-operationsactivity logList recent AI changes, with no saved data exposed
rollback-operationundoUndo a single change
rollback-sessionundoUndo an entire AI session, back to where it started
